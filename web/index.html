Stop guessing. Know exactly when your video ends.

YouTube Video Ends At adds a live ends at HH:MM:SS timestamp directly in the player so you can schedule tasks, breaks, or meetings without mental math.

Get Extension View Demo

Features

Instant End Time

See an immediate end timestamp next to YouTube's native time display.

Live Accuracy

Updates continuously while playback changes, pauses, or speed adjustments happen.

Lightweight Design

No tedious setup or configuration required. It just runs.
